About Me

Hello! My name is Isabella Matczak.

I am currently a senior at the University of Wisconsin-Eau Claire, studying English rhetoric, journalism, and multimedia communications. My passion is storytelling: I am dedicated to spreading the truth and sharing diverse voices, ensuring they are heard.

I am the managing editor of our student newspaper, The Spectator, at which I received the David Gordon Reporting Scholarship and the “Most Diverse Portfolio” award. This is my goal, to represent people and stories from every corner.

Since January 2025, I have also held the technical writing intern position at Royal Credit Union Corporate Center, where I write and edit all company policies and procedures and improve user accessibility.

This summer, I will be attending a journalism program with ieiMedia in London, England, where I will gain valuable international reporting and audio broadcast experience.
